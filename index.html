The Problem

Cables are annoying. Older cars don’t support wireless Android Auto. And upgrading your head unit can cost hundreds — sometimes thousands.

Drivers want modern connectivity, but most cars on the road today don’t have it. Every drive starts with plugging in, untangling cables, and fighting with outdated systems.

The Solution

AUTOFLOW is a tiny plug-and-play device that adds wireless Android Auto to ANY car.

  • Plug it in once.
  • Hide it in your console or glove box.
  • Your phone connects wirelessly every time you start the car.

No tools. No wiring. No installation. No dealership. Just seamless wireless Android Auto, every drive.

How It Works

AUTOFLOW quietly bridges your phone and your car’s head unit:

  • Your car thinks AUTOFLOW is a wired Android Auto device.
  • Your phone connects to AUTOFLOW wirelessly.
  • AUTOFLOW passes everything through to your existing head unit.
  • You get full Android Auto — wirelessly, every time.

Fast. Stable. Automatic. Once it’s plugged in, you don’t have to think about it again.

FAQ

Will it work with my car?

If your car supports wired Android Auto, AUTOFLOW is designed to make it wireless. Compatibility details will be listed clearly on the Kickstarter page and here on AUTOFLOWUSA.com.

Do I need tools or professional installation?

No. AUTOFLOW is plug-and-play. You connect it once to your car’s existing Android Auto USB port, tuck it away, and you’re done.

Will it drain my battery?

No. AUTOFLOW is designed to draw less power than a typical USB phone charger and only runs when your car’s system is active.
