How AUTOFLOW works

AUTOFLOW sits between your Android phone and your car’s head unit. It draws power from the 12V socket, creates a dedicated Wi‑Fi Direct link to your phone, and presents itself to the car as a wired Android Auto device—without ever touching the USB port.

1. Plug into 12V

Insert AUTOFLOW into your vehicle’s 12V socket. It powers on automatically with the ignition—no cables, no extra adapters.

Zero install

2. Pair your phone once

Connect your Android phone to AUTOFLOW via Wi‑Fi Direct. The secure link is remembered, so future drives connect automatically.

Instant reconnection

3. Car sees “wired” Android Auto

AUTOFLOW’s USB Gadget Emulation Engine presents a virtual wired Android Auto device to your head unit, unlocking full Android Auto—wirelessly.

USB port stays free
